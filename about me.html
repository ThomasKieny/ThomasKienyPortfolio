Get to know me

I'm a data analyst and marketing professional who has held positions as product manager and project manager in the pharmaceutical and electrical industries, as well as in communications agencies. I began a professional reconversion early in 2023 to throw myself fully into the world of data, and more specifically data analysis.

My passion for data in general has always been present. I've always enjoyed trying to make figures speak for themselves. The assignments I've enjoyed the most so far are those that have enabled me to analyze the commercial performance of products when I worked as a product manager, or those that have led me to study the results of digital campaigns in my current position. Trying to understand what's behind the data, to improve the product or service on offer, and exceed targets. Finding that little something, hidden in the immensity of the data collected, that can make a significant difference : that's what motivates me to give my best.

Today, through this portfolio, I'm going to show you how I can be a real asset as a data analyst through my strategic thinking, analytical skills using Excel, SQL, Python and Power BI and curiosity about different topics.
